Why I programme?

My interest in programming began in 2012, when, during an electromedicine course, I was introduced to PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers). I fell in love with the concept of writing some lines of code on a PC, pressing "run," and, magically, a piston would move.

Shortly after, I discovered Arduino MicroControllers, learned C and electronics, and, as they say, the rest is history.

The Magic of over Complicating!

When a few years ago my PC's hard drive... "retired", taking with it a lot of my cats photos... So I decided to build a NAS so that this wouldn't happen again!

After months of research and a budget overrun worthy of a corruption scandal, I purchased (and built) my second PC, creating a "server" on which I still run ProxMox VE and a collection of VMs. This gives me the privilege of being able to try out all kinds of Operating Systems without much hassle.
